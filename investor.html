Two-Stage AI Pipeline

Not hallucinated suggestions — real, sourced intel from live web research

1

Perplexity Research Agent (sonar-pro)

Searches Eater, The Infatuation, Reddit, TikTok, TimeOut, Serious Eats with domain filtering and recency filtering (last 30 days) — returns real restaurants with source citations

↓
2

User Database Cross-Reference

Matches research against tier rankings, visit history, and taste profile — no duplicate suggestions

↓
3

Claude Sonnet 4

Personalizes research with your taste profile using smart context. Two modes: Discover (research-first) and Search (database-first). Includes Resy & OpenTable booking links
