# TasteBuddy

**Your Buddy, with your Taste.**

TasteBuddy is a personal restaurant diary and AI food-discovery app — conceived and built by Colin Chia.

Instead of scrolling through notes or trying to remember where you ate six months ago, TasteBuddy gives you one place to track every spot you've loved, want to try, or are planning to visit — plus an AI that actually knows your taste to help you find your next one.

**Track your favorites.** Organize places into your own categories — Breakfast, Dinner, Ramen Night, whatever you like — and rank them across six tiers: **S, A, B, C, Next Up,** and **TBE** ("To Be Eaten"). Your S-tier is settled head-to-head in quick pairwise match-ups.

**Check in without the hassle.** Check in the moment you sit down, put your phone away, and get reminded to finish the details later — occasion, party size, spend, notes, and photos. No more "oh, I forgot to track that."

**Ask your TasteBuddy AI.** Not sure where to eat tonight? Ask in plain English — "best ramen near me" or "where should I take my parents for a special occasion?" It runs a live web research pass, grounds every suggestion in real places *and* your own rankings, and gives honest booking help (the real reservation link plus a reminder — never a fabricated one).

**See your taste take shape.** The **TasteBoard** dashboard charts your visits, cuisines, and milestones; your **Tastie Score** rolls it into one number out of 100; and your **Tastie Persona** is a shareable taste profile with a Taste DNA radar and a computed archetype.

**Eat like your Buddies.** Follow friends, share your AI, and browse curated personas — the Sushi Snob, the Spice Seeker, the Midnight Muncher, the Sommelier.

TasteBuddy is for people who care about where they eat and want to be intentional about it — whether you're a casual diner trying to remember the good spots, or a dedicated foodie building your definitive tier list.
