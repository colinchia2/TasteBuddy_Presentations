⚙️ How it's built — a RAG pipeline

Retrieval-Augmented Generation, grounded twice over — your own ranking data and live external research.

Claude Haiku classifies your intent → the engine retrieves your taste profile, a live Perplexity sonar-pro research pass, and real Google Places candidates → Claude Sonnet 4.6 synthesizes the answer → a final quality check.

✓ Recs & coordinates come only from real candidates — no fabricated places, no made-up reservation windows.
