🍴 Custom Categories & Rankings

The Tiering System

A structured hierarchy for every restaurant you visit

Tier Definitions

S
The Best of the Best Ranked 1 through N via pairwise comparison
A
Great Worth revisiting — a reliable go-to
B
Good Solid choice, nothing extraordinary
C
Below Average Overrated or disappointing
TBE
To Be Eaten Unranked wishlist — haven't visited yet

🏆 Why S-Tier Matters

S-Tier uses pairwise comparison (merge sort) for objective ranking. No guessing, no vibes — every position is earned through head-to-head matchups.
